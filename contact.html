FAQs:

(Click the questions to view the answers)

Can I bring my children?

Yes, children are more than welcome here at Escape. Anyone under the age of 16 must be supervised by an adult.


What time are children allowed in?

Anytime before 8pm, Escape is over 18's only after 8pm.


If I'm a member, does my guest still have to pay the guest fee?

Yes, membership covers entry for the member themselves. Guests will still have to pay the Â£3 guest fee.


What time is last entry?

Last entry is always 45 minutes before closing time, so if we close at 10pm, last entry is 9.15pm.


Are you wheelchair accessible?

Unfortunately not, due to our location on the 3rd floor of a grade 2 listed building, we are unable to be wheelchair accessible.


Does my guest fee count towards the table charge?

No, guest fees are seperate from table rates. Our guest fee is an entry fee to the venue itself.


Do you have customer parking?

We don't have parking at our venue, although there are several parking options nearby. On street paid parking outside the front of our venue, Intu shopping centre car park, or a nearby Ringo paid car park.


What is the difference between British and American pool?

British pool uses a smaller table with a green cloth and red and yellow balls. American pool uses a larger table with a blue cloth, and multi-coloured balls that can also be used as spots and stripes.
